Three Colored Beach

Tiga Warna or three colored beach is a beach with three colors gradation, due to the different depth by the sea. Graded water of blue and green as well as white sandy beach becomes the reason this beach named as such.

Tiga Warna beach is a part of Protected Mangrove and Coral Reef Conservation, located in Sendangbiru Region, Malang, East Java.

Singosari Temple

Singosari temple, also known as Kendedes temple, was founded in honor to King Kertanegara, the last king of Singosari dynasty who died in 1292 AD. This temple has saved the historical story that always remember by Malang public.

The temple is located on Jalan Kertanegara, Candirenggo village, Singosari district, about 10 kilometres north from Malang city

Coban Rondo

Coban Rondo waterfall is a beautiful water fall on the slope of Mount Panderman. The height of waterfall is about 60 meter. Visitor can find and enjoy beautiful panorama.

The waterfall is located at Pandesari village, Pujon District(about 32 km away to the West from Malang).

Wonosari Tea Plantation

This plantation pleasure gives a special and peaceful impression, beautiful panorama of tea plantation. The plantation is situated at an area on the slope of Mount Arjuno.

Wonosari tea plantation is located 30 KM north of Malang, precisely in Lawang District that lies on the foot of Mount Arjuno.

China Goa Beach

The original name of this beach is Pantai Indah Rowo, but change to china goa beach because the event had occurred the death of a Chinese who was imprisoned in the cave that is in the coastal area.

Besides popular with the cave, this beach also presents a rare natural phenomenon, namely a wave intersects not rerun from three directions, south, east and west.

China Goa beach is a beach on the southern coast located in the hamlet Tumpak Awu, Sitiarjo Village, District Sumbermanjing Wetan, Malang, East Java.

Boon Pring Andeman

Boon Pring Andeman is an ecotourism with many bamboo trees in this area. On this 36.8 hectare land, visitors will see the green of the tourist area with the majority of bamboo trees.

Boon Pring is located in Sanankerto Village, Turen District, Malang Regency.

Asmara Bay Beach

The beach is huge and suitable for bringing family and children. Asmara Bay Beach has a long coastline. There are quite a number of coral islands scattered along the coastline. In addition to playing on the beach, there is a hilltop to see Asmara Bay landscape.

The location is in Tambakrejo Village, Sumbermanjing Wetan District, Malang Regency, East Java

Malang Night Paradise

Malang Night Paradise combines aspects of education, art and technology in making it a tourist attraction. The number of educational facilities is a distinct advantage for this park.

Malang Night Paradise is located in Graha Kencana Raya Street number 66 Karanglo, Balearjosari, Blimbing, Malang City, East Java
